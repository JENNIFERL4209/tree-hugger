Forest

Are you having a hard time focusing? Is it because you are always on your phone? Well, Forest will come to rescue you! Forest is an app that forbids you to use your phone while you are planting a tree. Each time you plant a tree, you will earn coins to plant a real tree on Earth. This is a great way to improve our environment on Earth and of course, FOCUS!

Forest allows you to plant trees from 10 minute to 120 minute. During this duration, you may earn 3 to 43 coins. You can plant different kinds of trees based on your preference of tree species. You need to buy the trees first! :) You may also save up your coins to plant a real tree for 2500 coins. It sounds very simple right? Then get started! Just plant some trees and earn some coins. Note: If you use your phone when planting your trees, then your tree will die :(

FAQ

1. How does these companies/organizations earn the money to plant trees?

  • Ecosia is a search engine, so it gains money from search ads. It takes 80% of that profit and gives it away to tree supporting programs.
  • Forest on the other hand, has partnered up with other non-profit organization called Trees for The Future. This non-profit organization collect donations to fund its actions.

    • 2. Where are the trees planted?

  • For Ecosia, this varies and is all based on the data they collect. They do research on where in the world planting trees would be the most beneficial and funding the tree supporting program’s needs to plant trees.
  • Forest, doesn’t decide where the trees are planted, instead they leave that decision to Tree for The Future, who mainly focus on Africa.

    • 3. How does planting trees impact you positively?

  • Trees are beneficial to you because they provide you with oxygen that you breathe in everyday. Plus with the pollution in today’s world, trees are a great way to clean the air, giving you fresher and cleaner air.

    • 4. Why should we plant trees?

  • Planting trees is important because it is what helps us sustain the agriculture that produce a large amount of our fresh food.
  • Agroforestry, a farming technique, is when many trees are grown around or among a group of crops on a piece of land.
  • This technique allows trees to strengthen crops by making the crop’s soil more fertile. In addition, it allows trees to protect crops by lowering the the effect wind has on crops.
